Ignition Pay is a full-stack, cross-platform Stellar wallet that delivers a
native-grade payment experience on Android, iOS, and web from a single
codebase.

- Send and receive XLM, USDC, and any Stellar-issued asset instantly
- Manage trustlines, fund accounts, and review transaction history
- Deposit and withdraw fiat through regulated anchors (SEP-6 / SEP-24 / SEP-31)
- Invoke Soroban smart contracts directly from your wallet
- Biometric unlock, deep links, and offline-resilient transaction drafts

Ignition Pay is open source and non-custodial: you control your keys, and
every contract and API powering the app is auditable.
